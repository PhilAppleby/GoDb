Notes on GoDARTS genomic data quality control, assay types and imputation

General checks on pre-imputation data:
  • All data placed on the forward strand and at Genome Reference Consortium Build 37 (GRCh37 / hg19)
  • SNPs annotated with dbSNP rsid's (dbSNP 138)
  • Variants (markers) checked for call-rate of 95% and above
  • Samples checked for call-rates of 95% and above
  • Inheritance By Descent checks for relatedness in samples
  • Checks for concordance in sex of samples
Final checks prior to imputation:
  1. Base position and chromosome match with the HRC reference panel
  2. Strand Check
  3. Reference allele match
  4. Remove variants with allele frequency difference > 0.2 between GoDARTS data and reference panel
  5. Remove palindromic SNPs with Frequency > 0.4
  6. Remove duplicate markers
  7. Check indels and remove
